# Sozu Capital Payment Plug-in: Seamless Crypto-Enhanced Payments for E-Commerce

## Overview

Sozu Capital's payment plug-in revolutionizes e-commerce payments by blending traditional banking convenience with the efficiency and yields of stablecoin technology. Designed for traditional online merchants, our non-custodial, no-KYC solution allows customers to pay via familiar bank transfers or cards, while merchants receive funds in USDC stablecoins deposited into a high-yield liquid vault. Enjoy instant settlements, passive earnings, and effortless fiat withdrawals—all without lockup periods or hidden fees on core services. Outpace competitors like Fintoc with lower costs, faster access, and built-in yields that turn idle funds into revenue.

## Key Features

- **Familiar Customer Experience**: Customers pay using any bank account or card, just like standard transfers—no crypto knowledge required. Our widget integrates seamlessly into platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, or custom sites, pre-filling details for quick checkouts.
- **Stablecoin Receipts for Merchants**: Payments convert instantly to USDC, a secure, dollar-pegged stablecoin, minimizing volatility risks.
- **High-Yield Liquid Vault**: Funds auto-deposit into a non-custodial DeFi vault earning competitive yields (e.g., 5-10% APY via lending protocols like Aave). Fully liquid—no lockups; withdraw or spend anytime.
- **Instant Bank Withdrawals**: Off-ramp USDC to fiat directly to your bank account in seconds, powered by trusted providers.
- **No KYC Onboarding**: Privacy-first design skips lengthy verifications, speeding up merchant setup while maintaining compliance through optional tools.
- **API & Widget Integration**: Easy-to-install widget and robust APIs for refunds, payouts, and transaction matching—mirroring traditional services but on blockchain rails.

## How It Works

1. **Customer Pays**: At checkout, the customer selects bank transfer or card; our system handles the transaction via open banking or card networks.
2. **Instant Conversion**: Funds convert to USDC and deposit into your non-custodial vault.
3. **Earn Yields**: Idle balances automatically earn high yields through secure DeFi staking—principal untouched.
4. **Withdraw Anytime**: Need cash? Off-ramp to your bank instantly; no delays like next-day settlements.
5. **Merchant Dashboard**: Track transactions, yields, and withdrawals in a simple interface.
